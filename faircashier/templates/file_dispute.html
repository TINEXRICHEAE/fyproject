✅ Auto-Refund Eligible

If you haven't confirmed delivery (cleared the payment), you'll receive an automatic refund when you file a dispute.

  • Instant refund to your wallet
  • No admin review needed
  • Funds available immediately

⚠️ Admin Review Required

If you already confirmed delivery, your dispute will be escalated to admin for review.

  • Admin will review evidence
  • May take 2-5 business days
  • Decision is final

💡 Tips for Filing

  • Be specific and detailed in your description
  • Include timeline of events
  • Mention any attempts to contact seller
  • Upload clear evidence if available
  • Be honest and accurate
